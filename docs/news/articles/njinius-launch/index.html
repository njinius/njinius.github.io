NJinius Launch

We are going live!#

NJinius has been created by a group of friends working together on several projects in recent months. We've been trying to grow our networks by meeting new people and sharing ideas. So we thought the most effective way to do this is to combine all our contacts in one ecosystem and healthily see that flourish via friends. Doing it this way ensures the people in the community expand outwards via trusted connections.

We thought of a quirky name for the community, "NJinius" - as a play on words.

We would want to kick off discussions about many exciting topics, from business strategy, coding challenges to personal development, mental health, book reviews and much more.

Our primary source of communication will be via Slack, a business communication tool used around the world. It is straightforward to set up and use, so anyone can register and get going quickly. We're also finalising the NJinius website that details who we are and what we stand for.

If you'd be interested in helping us grow, please click here, and follow the simple steps to join the community.

You will receive an invite to our Slack channel, where you can meet everyone and start making new connections! When you get the invite, be sure to jump on the introduction channel and tell us a little about yourself.
