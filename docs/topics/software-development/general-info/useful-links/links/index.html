Useful Links

Tutorials#

The NJinius site contains a software tutorials section covering a range of software development tutorials on several technologies, software development principles and programming languages.

Tutorials are grouped under one of two categories, text tutorials or video tutorials. NJinius community members create all tutorials. See the links below.

  • Video tutorials
  • Text tutorials

For a better experience, we encourage you to join our community slack workspace, where you can communicate directly with the creators of these tutorials (or other members) and get answers to any questions you might have.

Discussions#

Slack#

Slack is the central communication medium for the community. You don't have to get left out of the conversation. You can join in any discussion, from business topics to software, books, and even general chat.

  • Join our slack

Live Sessions#

We host live workshops, tutorials and discussions in many different subject areas driven by community members. All community members are welcome to participate. Sessions are recorded and published on the website. Links to the schedule and recorded sessions can be found below.

  • Schedule
  • Recorded sessions

Challenges#

When the NJinius community are faced with a technical coding problem, we believe the best approach is to share and solve the problem together. We aim to collaborate and develop tangible solutions shared within the NJinius and broader open-source community. To view active challenges or for instructions on creating or participating in any challenge, visit the link below.
