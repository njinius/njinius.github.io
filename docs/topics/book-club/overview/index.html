Welcome

Book Club#

Welcome to the NJinius Reading Group. A platform for reading, dissecting and sharing perspectives on books. Discussions are summarised and recorded on the Njinius website, providing condensed reading material on every book's key concepts.

We welcome readers and writers alike to join and participate in our discussions

Mission#

  • To bring together a group of like-minded readers of all levels to dissect books on various topics covered by NJinius.
  • To extract and capture every book's key concepts to produce quick and easy to consume content.

How does the book club work#

  1. Two books are selected by the NJinius community members on slack every month.

    a. Readers make suggestions on the books they would like discussed.

    b. A poll is created for the suggested books.

    c. The top two books are selected. A random picker is used in the event of a tie.

  2. Timelines are formed based on the estimated reading time, where chapters are evenly split for discussion

    a. The schedule can be found on the Live Sessions section of this website.

    b. Earlier sessions can be found on the Previous Sessions section of this website.

How to participate#

  1. Join the NJinius slack workspace, and select the book club channel.

  2. Visit the Live Sessions page and click on the zoom link to be part of the discussion.

  3. Watch recorded videos of past sessions by visiting the Previous Sessions page.

  4. Find an overview of a book and a summary of all discussions on this section of the NJinius website.

    a. The purpose of this is to extract all valuable information and create a condensed set of information that captures the fundamental concepts of every book. The aim is to make it quick and easy to consume knowledge from all books that have been discussed.
