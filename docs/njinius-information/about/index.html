About

NJinius#

Welcome to the NJinius community, where iron sharpens iron!

NJinius was created to help people of all ability levels develop their understanding of software development, business, leadership and much more. We created a community of like-minded individuals who seek to expand and develop a 360 knowledge of their respective industries. We hold workshops, tutorials and discussions in many different subject areas for this purpose, offering a relaxed environment to learn and develop new skills.

NJinius is not limited to work and industry; this is a space designed to nurture creativity, personal development, friendships and collaborations. Our blog pages offer every user an opportunity to express their interests and ideas.

We also encourage you to share and discuss your work freely. The community is welcome to challenge each other's ideas and perspectives. However, please treat everyone with respect. We urge you to remember that this is a platform to help each other grow, collaborate, and inspire in all your interactions.

The community spans multiple areas of development. Some of these areas include:

Topics#

Select an area you would like to learn and develop. Your selected topic may be business, technology, leadership or any other available space. Hover over the "Getting Started" text in the Navigation bar to view and select from our list of topics.

Tutorials#

These are tutorials the community have created for your benefit. Topics range from programming to business strategy and many more. Both interactive text and video tutorials are available. See the following:

  • Video tutorials
  • Text tutorials

Slack#

Slack is the central communication medium for the community. You don't have to get left out of the conversation. You can join in any discussion, from business topics to software, books, and even general chat. NJinius slack provides for all of these interactions. Enter the relevant channel, and you're good to go!

Github#

We have an open-source culture and encourage collaboration in the Github community. As the community grows, we'd like to expand our open source projects from simple coding challenges to community-driven refined libraries that others and companies can use

Live Sessions#

Why not join us for live sessions where we openly discuss several subjects, present tutorials and discuss our favourite books!

These are only a few areas of NJinius - We are a friendly community...#

So please join us, innovate, have fun and do something NJinius!
